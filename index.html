The Transfer Student

A young adult novel exploring systemic inequity through a speculative lens.

Follow Amara as she uncovers the hidden struggles inside schools across the world.

Each transfer challenges her understanding of education, privilege, and power.

When visions of the future appear, Amara realizes the stakes are higher than ever.

The fight for justice has already begun—will she find her way home in time?

About Nasharie

I am a writer, advocate, and aspiring attorney passionate about education equity and storytelling that sparks change. Growing up in an underfunded school district shaped my drive to reimagine broken systems. Now, through my work, my studies, and my stories, I am committed to amplifying overlooked voices and building a more just world.

"An infinite loop of growth, creativity, and voice — that's what my writing, and this space, represents."
