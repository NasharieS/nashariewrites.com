Support for Students

Whether you’re starting a new chapter in your educational journey or reflecting on how far you’ve come, I’m here to help.

Through Matriculate, I supported high school students in brainstorming, outlining, and polishing college personal statements. At Johns Hopkins University's Writing Center, I worked with undergraduate and graduate students across disciplines on personal statements for law school, medical school, and more.

No matter where you are in the process — from having no idea what to write to final edits — I'm excited to collaborate with you.

I also offer speaking engagements centered on educational equity, writing, and social advocacy.

How Can I Support You?

Personal Statement Help

Brainstorming, outlining, drafting, and final edits for college, law school, med school, and more.

Speaking Engagement

Workshops, panels, or keynotes on education equity, writing journeys, and student advocacy.
